Who Are We?

Free

Our website offers users the ability to create their own wishlist
of current and upcoming games at no expense.

Story

We are Dev Team 1, students of ECPI University, and we created
RetroSto because there were no other free websites that offered gamers the ability to creat their own wishlist with games across any
of their preferred gaming platforms. As developers we sought to create exactly what we would want as gamers, and we're proud to have succeeded.

Mission

Our goal is to aid gamers in cataloging and referencing their favorite
and most anticipated games across any of their preferred gaming platform. The site was also designed to be as user friendly as possible, allowing our users to share their wishlist with friends and loved ones, be it for gift giving or even the connection that sharing our interests can bring about.
